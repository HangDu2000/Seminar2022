Seminar on Discrete Probability, Peking University, Fall 2022

Time: TBA

Location: TBA

Organizer: Jian Ding and Hang Du


Introduction: This is a seminar for junior and senior students who are interested in probability theory. Each Participant chooses a probability-related topic he or she is interested, and give presentation on the seminar. The topics may come from papers, lecture notes or books. After the presentations, participants can have discussions and enjoy free food. Given that most audiences are starters on probability theory, it is better for speakers to choose topics without many involved preliminaries. The most suitable ones are those which can be made self-contained in one or two lessons (2-4 hours). Junior students are strongly encouraged to take part in thie seminar, for enhancing the ability of handling advanced materials and making good presentation.
